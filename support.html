Daylight — Support

Help for the Daylight iOS app.

Daylight shows how much daylight is left today, the sun’s position across the sky, your sunrise and sunset times, and how the length of the day changes through the year.

Need help or have feedback?
Email daylight_app@proton.me and we’ll get back to you. Including your device model and iOS version helps us respond faster.

Frequently asked

“Use my location” isn’t working

Make sure location access is enabled: open the iOS Settings app → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Daylight, and allow access while using the app. You can also just search for your city instead.

How do I change my location?

Tap the gear icon, then your current location, and either search for a city or tap Use my location.

Are my sunrise/sunset times accurate?

Times are computed on your device from your location using standard astronomical formulas. They may differ by a minute or two from other sources, which can use slightly different definitions of sunrise/sunset.

Does Daylight collect my data?

No accounts, no ads, no tracking. See our Privacy Policy for details.
